Welcome! I work as a predoctoral Research Professional at both the Universidad de Los Andes and The University of Chicago. At Los Andes, I work with professor Raquel Bernal on projects related to labor economics and early childhood development. At UChicago, I work with professor Luis R. Martínez on political economy and development economics research.

My main interests are development economics, political economy, and labor economics, especially early childhood interventions.

I hold an M.A. and a B.A. in Economics from Universidad de los Andes in Bogotá, Colombia.
