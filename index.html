Welcome! I'm a third-year PhD student in Economics at The University of Chicago.

I’m interested in labor and behavioral economics in low-income communities. My current projects—some more real than others—focus on education, migration, family policy, and financial inclusion.

I hold an M.A. and a B.A. in Economics from Universidad de los Andes in Bogotá, Colombia.

You can find my CV here.
